About us:

We are a group of final-year Physics students at Durham University. This project was completed for the NHS as part of one of our modules.

This project was proposed by Dr Prakash Loganathan, consultant neonatologist at the James Cook University Hospital, Middlesbrough. Dr Loganathan works with neonatal patients who often require supplemental oxygen to be able to maintain their oxygen levels.
